Consulting Process

Here is an overview of the process.

Situational Analysis
First Step: Situational Analysis

First we need to gather your background information and based those information, we'll provide a roadmap on for our job-hunting journey.


Group Review
Second Step：Group Review

Our committee group will review your case and discuss for a viable plan.


Hunting Execution
Third Step: Hunting Execution

After we settle our plan, we will start to execute it. We will be constantly reviewing the procedure to update our plan and strategy.


Interview Preparation
Fourth Step: Interview Preparation

We will start preparing interview before you have one, which includes profile update, interview tips, mock interview and etc.


Post-Inteview
Fifth Step：Post-Interview

We will discuss the good and bad after each interview and what we can improve on the next one.


Accepting Offer
Sixth Step: Accepting Offer

We will discuss strategies on offer picking and offer negotiation.
